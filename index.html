About Sumadhwa Vijaya

Sumadhwa Vijaya is a Sanskrit biographical epic (mahakavya) composed by Narayana Panditacharya, a contemporary disciple of Sri Madhvacharya. Written in the 13th century CE, it chronicles the extraordinary life, teachings, and miracles of the founder of Dvaita Vedanta philosophy.

Key Features

  • 16 Cantos (Sargas): Comprehensive biography
  • Eyewitness Account: Written by a contemporary
  • Historical Value: Primary source on Madhva's life
  • Literary Excellence: Classical Sanskrit poetry
